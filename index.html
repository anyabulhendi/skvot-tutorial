FILMS ABOUT DESIGN

Films about designers and design are a great way to expand your professional horizons in a simple and easy way.

Helvetica

It's hard to imagine a movie all about one typeface unless it's about Helvetica. Designed in Switzerland over half a century ago by Max Miedinger and Eduard Hoffman, the sans-serif typeface quickly became recognizable - it appeared on corporate logos, transportation schemes and artwork. But the film is not so much about a specific typeface as it is about how typography affects our lives and shapes the space in which we live, from the United States to the United Kingdom.
